Daily Quiz Pro

Logos

 February 2022

Daily Quiz Pro is a daily quiz and blog site offering a wide range of topics and focuses on daily quiz content.

Creative Brief

This was a new logo creation and identity for the website. We wanted to use bright and playful colors to highlight all of the different categories of trivia.

I started with some sketches and then took them into Figma to finalize a logo.

Final Logo

Horizontal & Vertical logo layout B&W

The design process was short and brief with minimal revisions. We settled on the logo and created variations with an assigned color and logo for each.

Logo Animation

The client wanted to have a GIF that transitioned through all of the quiz categories to give users a quick glance at what they could expect.
