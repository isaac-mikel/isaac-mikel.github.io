Idaho Helicopters

Logos

 Feb 2022

This was my first helicopter project and it was a lot of fun to work through. The client had a logo but wanted to refresh it and make something more stylized for employee branded T-shirts.

Creative Brief

Client provided 4 icons and a compass shape that they wanted to include in the logo. They also wanted to add in a Bell 429 helicopter to one of the variations.

Round 1
First round of sketches

First round of sketches were sent over to the client and they gave feedback on the designs and a couple variations they wanted to see. Took the sketches into Figma and created vector logo variations.

Final
Final logo options

The client really loved the vector options sent over with their revisions made. They loved them all and wanted time to decide which one to use. I packaged up each logo file and sent them over for them to use and to send to a printer.
