LiveRez Conference Backgrounds

Illustration

 Dec 2020

In preperation for the LiveRez Partner Conference, we wanted to get people excited and engaged with the event. We wanted to put out a collection of custom illustrated backgrounds for our partners and customers to use on their own and during the online conference.

Creative Brief

We selected 7 locations that we wanted to illustrate. These locations were based off of major tourist areas and where our customers were located. I gathered references and started with a sketch for each location.

Locations: Blue Ridge, West Coast, East Coast, South, Pacific West, Hawaii, Midwest

Sketches

Final Backgrounds

After sketches were approved, I went into Illustrator and started tracing over each sketch location and then applied a color palette based off references to the vectors.
