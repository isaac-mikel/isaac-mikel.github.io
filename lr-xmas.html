LiveRez Xmas Backgrounds

Illustration

 Dec 2020

LiveRez was looking for a fun way to spice up company meetings during pandemic at home working. We wanted to create unique seasonal customized backgrounds for employees and customers to use.

Creative Brief

For the Christmas video background we wanted to capture the feeling of a small Christmas miniature scene. Starting with a sketch to outline the placement and arrangement of the scene keeping in mind to keep the objects of interest out of the center.

Final

After sketches were approved, I went into Illustrator and started tracing over the sketch and then adding in a color palette to the vectors.
