LiveRez

Logos

 September 2020

LiveRez is a vacation rental software company that is focused on quality customer support and helping property & vacation rental managers make more revenue.

Creative Brief
Previous logo 2018

I got to work on the logo redesign project with 2 other designers and a project manager to come up with a group of logos to tweak and fine tune down until we decided on the final logo.

We looked at the LiveRez brand and narrowed down a list of key attributes we wanted to highlight about the brand in the logo. This was the first round of sketches based on those attributes.

Logo Variations

The team went through multiple rounds of revisions and edits of the logo variations we had created. With each round we eliminated and combined logos to narrow down the list to choose from.
