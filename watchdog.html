Watchdog Security

Logos

 Feb 2021

Fun logo for a family project. Watchdog Security is a security app that allows AI to analyze your security camera feed to give you real time alerts.

Creative Brief

The client wanted the logo to be an icon of one of our family pets. We wanted to highlight their alert nature and give some personality to the branding.

Starting with sketches based off of reference photos.

Final
Final logo options

Worked on converting the sketches to a vector logo with a large and small application. Added in a color palette and wordmark for the final version.
