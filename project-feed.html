Intro

PROJECT: feed is philanthropic project created by LiveRez Vacation Rental Software to raise money for food and groceries for those in need.

Creative Brief

I was given creative freedom on this project and was asked to make a brand guide, logo, and tote mockups for the design to be printed and given out at conferences.

I created a number of sketches and we explored more with the design including fresh produce and food items surrounding the project name.
